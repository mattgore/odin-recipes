Taco

Mexican style tacos. Source: wikipedia.org

Description

The taco is a mexican food consisting of a tortilla, made with corn or flower, and a filling .

This filling can be meat, rice, vegetables, beans, cheese, or a combination of foods.

For this recipe, we will be making a carne asada taco with lime, cilantro, and onion.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Carne asada
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Salsa
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Warmn tortilla in pan, then place tortilla on plate.
  2. Place carne asada in the center of the tortilla.
  3. Top carne asada with chopped onion and cilantro.
  4. Pour salsa over taco.
  5. Serve with sliced lime.
  6. EAT! And enjoy!
