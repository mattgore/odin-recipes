Pepperoni Pizza

A homemade pepperoni pizza. Source: simplyrecipes.com

Description

The pizza is an italian dish consisting of bread, tomato sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

Some pizzas can include toppings like Italian sausage, olives, or anchovies.

For this recipe, we will be making a homemade personal pizza with pepperoni slices as a topping..

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough into a large circle.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough starting at the center.
  3. Top tomato sauce with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Place pepperoni slices over cheese.
  5. Bake in oven until center is cooked and crust is golden brown.
  6. Let cool, then slice into eighths.
  7. EAT! And enjoy!
