Cheeseburger

American-style hamburger with cheese. Source: epicurious.com

Description

The hamburger with cheese, also known as a cheeseburger, is a German-originated sandwich consisting of bread, beef, cheese, and in many cases, condiments.

Cheeseburgers can also contain lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.

For this recipe, we will be making a standard cheeseburger with ketchup and mayonnaise for condiments.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. ground beef
  • Sliced American cheese
  • Hamburger bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patty. Cook until center is slightly pink.
  2. Toast hamburger bun.
  3. Turn off heat on stove. Place slice of cheese on patty and let melt.
  4. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on toasted bun.
  5. Place patty on bun.
  6. EAT! And enjoy!
